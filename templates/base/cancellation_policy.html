Our Cancellation Policy

At LuxStay, we understand that plans can change. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair to both our guests and our property owners. Please review the following policy carefully before making your reservation.

Standard Cancellation Policy

1 Flexible Cancellation (7+ days before check-in)

Cancel at least 7 days before your check-in date to receive a full refund (minus any service fees).

2 Moderate Cancellation (3-6 days before check-in)

Cancel between 3-6 days before check-in to receive a 50% refund of the accommodation fee (service fees non-refundable).

3 Strict Cancellation (Within 48 hours)

Cancellations made less than 48 hours before check-in will not receive a refund. No-shows will be charged the full reservation amount.

Special Cases & Exceptions

1 Peak Season & Special Events

During peak seasons or special events, a stricter cancellation policy may apply (14-day cancellation for full refund). This will be clearly stated at booking.

2 Long-term Stays (28+ days)

For stays of 28 nights or more: Cancel within 48 hours of booking for full refund. After 48 hours, first month is non-refundable.

3 Extenuating Circumstances

In cases of serious illness, death, or natural disasters, please contact us immediately. Documentation may be required for exceptions.

Refund Process

1 Processing Time

Refunds typically process within 7-10 business days. The time it takes for the refund to appear in your account depends on your financial institution.

2 Service Fees

Service fees are non-refundable unless the cancellation is due to an error on our part or extenuating circumstances.

3 Credit vs. Original Payment

Refunds are issued to the original payment method. If this isn't possible, we may issue a credit for future stays.

How to Cancel Your Reservation

1 Online Cancellation

Log in to your LuxStay account, go to "My Trips," select the reservation, and click "Cancel Reservation."

2 Email Cancellation

Send cancellation requests to cancellations@luxstay.com with your reservation number and details.

3 Phone Support

Call our customer service at +1 (800) 555-LUXS (5897) for assistance with cancellations.

By making a reservation with LuxStay, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to this Cancellation Policy.
